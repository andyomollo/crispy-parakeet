Did you know that beer is one of the oldest recorded recipes in the world? Archaeologists discovered that ancient Sumerians were brewing beer over 5,000 years ago, and they even had a goddess of beer named Ninkasi.

In fact, one of the earliest written texts in human history is a hymn to Ninkasi that doubles as a beer recipe—proof that from the very beginning, people have been serious about perfecting their brew!
